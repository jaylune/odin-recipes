Curry Udon

source: Just One Cookbook

Description

A wonderful combo with thick noodles and a heavy, rich soup that will leave you with a fulfilling full feeling.

Ingredients

  • Protein of Choice (Pork Loin,Chicken, etc.)
  • Onions
  • Green Onions
  • Udon Noodles
  • Dashi
  • Japanese curry roux
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Make the dashi in the pot.
  2. Stir fry the onion and protein in a separate frying pan.
  3. Add the dashi and curry roux to the pan and simmer over medium-low heat.
  4. Boil the udon noodles in a large pot and drain.
  5. Serve the noodles and pour the curry soup on top.
