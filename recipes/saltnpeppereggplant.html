Salt and Peper Eggplant

source: MyEagerEats

Description

A Chinese takeout classic and a lovely, delicious way to get your daily intake of veggies.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Onions
  • Chilis
  • Chinese 5 Spice Seasoning
  • Corn Flour

Steps

  1. Cut eggplant french-fry style and salt.
  2. Leave out for 5 minutes to dry. Dab with paper towel.
  3. Coat eggplant in seasoning and cornflour.
  4. Toss onion and chilis into the pan. After a few seconds, add the eggplant.
  5. Season the eggplant again.
  6. Serve.
